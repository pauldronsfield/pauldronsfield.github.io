Creative Edge Photography provides professional commercial photography in Essex, Chelmsford, London and the Home Counties.

Established in 1998, the company has nearly twenty years experience in the commercial photography sector. Creative Edge Photography supplies business portrait and headshot photography, interior and property photography, studio packshot photography, and product photography to the public and private sectors.

Anyone can point a camera and take a photograph but good, well lit and composed images taken by a professional commercial photographer and used within your advertising and promotional material, make a strong impression. Creative Edge Photography gives clients photographs they can be proud of, enabling them to present their business and services with confidence.

Creative Edge Photography has a satisfied group of customers including businesses in the manufacturing and service sectors, local councils and schools, restaurants and hotels.
