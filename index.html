We provide commercial photography in Essex, Chelmsford, London and the Home Counties.

Established in 1998, the company has many years experience in the photographic business, supplying corporate, editorial, property and public relations photography to the public and private sectors.

Anyone can point a camera and take a picture but good, well lit and composed images taken by a professional photographer and used within your advertising and promotional material, make a strong impression. We aim to give clients images they can be proud of, enabling them to present their business and services with confidence.

Creative Edge Photography has a satisfied group of customers including businesses in the manufacturing and service sectors, local councils and schools, restaurants and hotels.
